Setup Checklist

  1. 1 Create a Twilio account at twilio.com and verify your email.
  2. 2 Buy a phone number with Voice capability (Phone Numbers → Buy a Number). Choose a local or toll-free number.
  3. 3 Add your credentials to .env:
    TWILIO_ACCOUNT_SID=ACxxxxx…
    TWILIO_AUTH_TOKEN=your_token
    TWILIO_PHONE_NUMBER=+1XXXXXXXXXX
  4. 4 Set APP_URL in .env to your public deployment URL (e.g. your Replit or Railway URL). This is used to generate the webhook URLs above.
  5. 5 In the Twilio console go to Phone Numbers → Manage → Active Numbers → your number.
    Under Voice & Fax set "A Call Comes In" to the Voice webhook URL above (HTTP POST).
    Under Call Status Changes paste the Status callback URL above.
  6. 6 Click Test Twilio Connection above — it should report your account name, account status, and confirm the phone number is found.
  7. 7 (Production) Set TWILIO_VALIDATE=true in .env to enable request-signature verification — this ensures only real Twilio calls reach your webhooks.
Security tip: Twilio request validation is currently disabled. Set TWILIO_VALIDATE=true in your .env before going live to prevent spoofed webhook calls.
